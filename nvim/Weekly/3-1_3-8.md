## Built-in Autocomplete

Whole lines                                | CTRL-X CTRL-L |
keywords in 'thesaurus', thesaurus-style   | CTRL-X CTRL-T |
keywords in 'dictionary'                   | CTRL-X CTRL-K |
File names                                 | CTRL-X CTRL-F |
Spelling suggestions                       | CTRL-X s      |
